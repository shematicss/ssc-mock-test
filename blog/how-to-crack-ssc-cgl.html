60-day daily plan

Structure: 2 hrs concept + 1 hr practice + 40 min revision + 20 min error log.

Days 1–20: Arithmetic + Number System (core). Days 21–35: Algebra + Geometry/Mensuration. Days 36–45: Trigonometry + DI + English vocab/grammar. Days 46–60: Full-length mocks + targeted revision.

Mock test strategy

  • Attempt 3 sectional + 2 full-length mocks per week.
  • Analyse within 24 hours—note wrong concepts in an **Error Log**.
  • Redo the same mock after 7 days to measure retention.

Common mistakes to avoid

Over-learning theory, skipping analysis, and not revising high-yield formulas. Keep a one-page formula sheet and update it daily.
